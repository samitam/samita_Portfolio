About

Notes — about.md

Hi, I'm Samita 👋

I'm an iOS developer in Aldie, Virginia, passionate about building AI-powered apps with SwiftUI and OpenAI's APIs. Most of my work lives at the intersection of native mobile development and practical AI — turning raw notes into structured content, scanning physical documents and extracting clean, usable data, or building conversational interfaces that feel like part of the OS.

I care deeply about getting the architecture right before features ship. Clean MVVM separation, async-first networking, code that's easy for the next person (often future me) to pick up and extend. Security and privacy aren't afterthoughts — Face ID, Keychain, and on-device processing are defaults, not features I add later.
